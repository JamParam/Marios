Organic fruit and vegetables delivered with a big smile

At Mario’s, we believe in the quality of our produce and the excellence of our service. Our organic fruit and vegetables are nurtured by Victorian farmers, delivered early each morning to our market, packaged with in our freshness sealing delivery kits, and transported from our door to yours. No pesticides, residues or chemical-based fertilisers are used to produce our fruit or vegetables, and this is evident in their taste, texture and quality. We strive to provide the best service as well, with our friendly deliverers and shop staff. Whether you're buying for yourself, for your family or for the customers at your restaurant, with Mario’s, you will always be satisfied.
